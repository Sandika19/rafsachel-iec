Our Mission

Make it possible for individuals to live healthier lives and to contribute to a more sustainable world.
We all want high-quality products for our homes and workplaces as consumers. There is a way to meet that requirement without disregarding the effects that product production has on the environment. Rafsachel is here for of it.
By selecting and selling only non-toxic, eco-friendly materials, providing green design advice, and disseminating the most recent knowledge on green construction methods and products, Rafsachel is dedicated to fostering healthier living environments.
Our objective is to provide customers with informed options and genuine value by being open and honest about the harmful chemicals that many commonly used, conventionally manufactured items contain.
In order to bring these products to market at the most competitive rates, Rafsachel conducts research, finds, and analyzes safer, healthier, higher performing, and more ecologically responsible alternatives to popular and conventionally designed products.

What We Offer

  • To serve our customers with transparency and an honest evaluation

  • Recognize that the integrity and transparency of the data we obtain from our vendors or that is made publicly available constitute the foundation of brand trust.

  • To enhance our environment via planetary stewardship for the benefit of all.

  • To encourage responsible consumption.

  • To promote sustainable community development.
